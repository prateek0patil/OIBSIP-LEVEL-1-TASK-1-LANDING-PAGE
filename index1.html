ABOUT US

We are a group of people with decades of experience in growing and selling mangoes. We know what a good mangoes looks like and how they are grown. This essential fruit requires a temperate climate, loamy soil that is rich in organic matter apart from needing proper drainage and aeration facilities. Lucky for us we live in India which is proudly known as paradise of earth is also home to temperate fruits like apple, oranges for which the state is very famous across globe. The main factor which influence temperate fruit bearing trees is soil, climate and environment which are highly favorable and unparalleled in the province of South India. Indian mangoes have unique look, taste, flavor, size and color.

VARIETIES

Totapuri

Mango looks like a parrot's beak. Hails from Karnataka,AP and Telangana.

Hapus

Native to Maharashtra, this variety also grown in Gujarat and Karnataka.

Sindhuri

This is very good for preparing shakes, as the pulp has a good yellow colour.




Banginapalli

This mango variety is produced in Banaganapalle of Kurnool district, Andhra Pradesh.


Ratnagiri

This mango is found in the Maharashtra region of Ratnagiri, Devgarh, Raigad, and Konkan.





Raspuri

Largely grown and consumed in Old Mysuru of Karnataka, this variety is known as the Queen of Mangoes in India.
